WORDS OF THE DEAD — Improvement Suggestions
===========================================
Items marked [DONE] are already implemented. Others are proposed but not yet built.

The single biggest learning lever was that words used to be picked at random with no
memory of what the player struggled with; the spaced-repetition system (A1-A3) changes
the app from a "vocab arcade" into a "vocab trainer".


A. LEARNING EFFECTIVENESS
-------------------------
[DONE] 1. Per-word mastery tracking + spaced repetition (Leitner).
          Each word has a Leitner box (0-5) with a due date, persisted across launches
          to ~/Library/Application Support/WordsOfTheDead/learning_profile.json.
          The scheduler resurfaces overdue/missed words and rests mastered ones, mixing
          in new words so the player keeps learning.

[DONE] 2. Prioritize missed words within a session.
          A wrong guess or a timeout re-queues the word to reappear 2-3 spawns later in
          the same session for immediate reinforcement.

[DONE] 3. Mastery levels per word (new -> learning -> known).
          Stage is derived from the Leitner box: New (0 attempts), Learning (boxes 1-3),
          Known (box >= 4). Reaching "Known" requires 4 correct answers that are
          necessarily spaced apart (the box-3 review interval is a full day). Shown as a
          badge on the reveal card and as a "Mastered: N / total" HUD counter.

4. Mix question types per word.
   Alternate modes for the SAME word (definition->word, word->definition, cloze,
   synonym) so the player recognizes it from multiple angles. Currently the mode is
   fixed by level parity (odd = definition, even = fill-in-the-blank).

5. Reverse direction.  (Implemented as a third rotating level type — see [DONE] note.)
[DONE] 5b. Reverse-direction level type.
   Levels now rotate through THREE kinds: (1) word shown, pick the definition;
   (2) fill-in-the-blank; (3) definition shown on the zombie, pick the WORD from four
   rotating candidates (press SPACE). This is the "reverse direction" variation.


B. ENGAGEMENT / COMPULSION LOOPS
--------------------------------
6. Persistent progress dashboard.
   Accuracy, best streak, and a full mastery bar. (A partial version exists: the HUD
   shows "Mastered: N / total", but there is no dedicated dashboard screen.)

7. Daily goal / streak calendar.
[DONE] 7. Daily goal / streak calendar.
   "Practice 20 words a day" with a consecutive-day streak counter and a 7-day activity
   calendar shown on the start and game-over screens. Practice counts (per day) persist to
   ~/Library/Application Support/WordsOfTheDead/daily_goal.json.

8. Combo / multiplier scoring.
[DONE] 8. Combo / multiplier scoring.
   Each kill scores base points + a speed bonus (more for killing a zombie higher up),
   multiplied by a combo that grows with the current streak (up to x5) and resets on a
   miss. Score and live combo multiplier are shown in the HUD.

9. End-of-run summary.
[DONE] 9. End-of-run summary.
   Game-over screen shows final score, zombies killed, accuracy, best streak, newly
   mastered words, and words to review.


C. GAMEPLAY FEEL & DIFFICULTY
-----------------------------
10. Smarter distractors.
    Use semantically close (or previously-confused) wrong answers instead of random
    same-part-of-speech entries, for better discrimination learning.

11. Difficulty tiers by word frequency.
[DONE] 11. Difficulty tiers (length/syllable proxy — no frequency corpus is bundled).
    Each word gets a difficulty tier (0 easiest … 3 hardest) from its length + estimated
    syllables. New words are introduced easiest-first, and harder tiers unlock as the
    player levels up (tier 0 at L1-2, then one more tier every two levels).

12. Adjustable / adaptive speed.
[DONE] 12. Adaptive speed (rubber-banding).
    Fall speed is nudged up or down by recent accuracy (last 8 outcomes) on top of the
    per-level curve, keeping difficulty near the player's ability.

13. "Boss" review rounds.
[DONE] 13. Boss review rounds.
    Every 4th level (once the player has words worth reviewing) is a faster "BOSS REVIEW"
    drawn only from struggling/missed words.


D. FEEDBACK & REINFORCEMENT
---------------------------
14. Show WHY you were wrong.
    On a miss, briefly flash the correct definition/word (a "miss reveal" to match the
    existing correct reveal). High-value quick win.

15. Audio / haptic cues.
    Distinct sounds for correct / wrong / streak / level-up.

16. Hint mechanic.
    A key to dim one wrong definition (odd levels) at the cost of score/speed.


E. QUALITY-OF-LIFE
------------------
17. Pause / resume.

18. Word journal / review screen.
    Browse learned words and their fun definitions outside of gameplay.

19. Settings.
    Toggle question modes, starting level, speed, and a colorblind-safe palette.


RECOMMENDED NEXT PICKS (impact per effort): #14 (miss reveal), #9 (end-of-run summary),
and #10 (smarter distractors).
